How to play Heavy Axe

Keys

  • Arrow Keys/Numpad - Move (4-way)
  • Space - Rest, pickup, or activate rune
  • d - Drop the Axe
  • 1-9 - Activate scrolls from your inventory
  • i - Use the crappy inventory menu to drop items
  • t - taunt enemies, range increases the longer you have been in a particular spot
A link at the bottom of your game with the seed will provide a link for the exact same dungeon. Send it to your friends, or try and beat your old fastest run!

Story

The gods have gone mad and has tormented your people long enough. It's up to you to enter their temple, make your way to the top, and destroy them. To accomplish this, you have been given a magical axe. It is capable of destroying anything - mortal or immortal. The downside... it is heavy as f***.

Gameplay and tactics

  • You deal Stamina damage per attack, with a minimum of 1
  • All monsters do 1 damage, unless specified otherwise
  • Pay attention to the maximum health of incoming monsters (right side), don't get trapped! You can't afford to waste turns taking damage
  • While carrying the axe, each step will drain your limited stamina. Since stamina determines the amount of damage you do, you must manage your steps carefully.
  • Use every trick in the book to taunt, cajole, trap, teleport, or otherwise draw out your enemies into your attacks.
  • Every time you strike down an enemy, your stamina replenishes fully... use this to chain as many attacks together as possible. (Without getting hit of course)
  • You can drop the Axe and move without losing stamina. Activating a Rune of Recall or reading a Scroll of Recall will summon the Axe back to you, smiting any enemies in the way. The axe will deal up to six damage, depending on how far it travels (more distance = more damage).
  • For tougher enemies, Recall will be a necessity if you want to kill them without being hit, because they have more than six health!
  • Advance to the next level by defeating all enemies
  • Taunting has a maximum effective distance. It will taunt all enemies equally, so be careful who you are waking up!

Scrolls

You can pick these up and use them via the hotkeys 1-9.

  • Scroll of Recall - Summons the Axe back to your possession, damaging any enemies along the way (think Thor's hammer, only text-based). The further it travels, the more damage it will do, up to a maximum of six.
  • Scroll of Teleport - Teleports you to a (supposedly) safe space on the level
  • Scroll of Sacrifice - Refills your stamina completely at the cost of 1 health. However, the scroll cannot kill you by taking your final health point
  • Scroll of Shield - Gives you a protective shield good against one hit. Does not stack.

Runes

Runes are activated by standing on them and RESTing

  • Rune of Recall - Same as a Scroll of Recall, except the rune is inexhaustible
  • Rune of Health - Will restore one health when activated, and then disappear. An invaluable rune.
  • Rune of Portal - All Runes of Portal are connected in pairs. Activating the rune will teleport you between them. What happens if an enemy gets in the way?
